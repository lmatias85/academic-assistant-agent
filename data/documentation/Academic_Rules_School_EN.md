# Academic Rules -- Academic System (School)

## 1. Student Academic Status

A student may be in one of the following academic statuses:

-   **REGULAR**
    -   May enroll in courses
    -   May take evaluations
    -   May receive grades
-   **FREE**
    -   May not enroll in new courses
    -   May not take regular evaluations

A student is considered **REGULAR** as long as they have at least one
active enrollment with REGULAR status.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2. Course Enrollment

### 2.1 General Conditions

A student may enroll in a course if:

-   The student is in **REGULAR** academic status
-   The course belongs to the current academic period
-   The student is not already enrolled in another course of the same
    subject during the same period

### 2.2 Enrollment Limits

-   A student may enroll in a maximum of **4 courses per term**
-   Enrollment in more than one course of the same subject at the same
    time is not allowed

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 3. Enrollment Regularity

Possible enrollment statuses:

-   ENROLLED
-   REGULAR
-   FREE
-   PASSED

### 3.1 Obtaining Regular Status

An enrollment becomes **REGULAR** when:

-   Attendance requirements are met
-   There are no active academic sanctions

### 3.2 Loss of Regular Status

An enrollment becomes **FREE** when:

-   Minimum attendance requirements are not met
-   The student abandons the course

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 4. Grades and Subject Approval

### 4.1 Grading Scale

-   Numeric grading scale: **0 to 10**
-   Minimum passing grade: **6**

### 4.2 Evaluation Result

-   Grade ≥ 6 → **PASSED**
-   Grade \< 6 → **FAILED**

### 4.3 Subject Approval

A subject is considered **PASSED** when the student obtains at least one
grade with PASSED result.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 5. Prerequisites

### 5.1 Definition

A subject may have one or more prerequisites that must be **passed
beforehand**.

### 5.2 Enrollment with Prerequisites

A student may enroll in a subject only if:

-   All prerequisite subjects are **PASSED**
-   REGULAR or FREE prerequisite statuses are not accepted

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 6. General Considerations

-   The system does not manage academic exceptions
-   All rules apply uniformly
-   Any situation not explicitly covered is considered not allowed
